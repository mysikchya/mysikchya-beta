The Keys to Effective Learning

Here are some fundamental keys to effective learning based on the principles discussed within the Memletics Accelerated Learning Manual

Achieving Your Ideal Learning State

o Healthy Physiology
o Healthy Cells
o Healthy Psychology
o Brain Training

The Learning Process

• Locate and identify the learning materials
• Exploration of your resources and topic content
• Prepare for memorization

Learning Techniques
1. Associate with the knowledge of memory to learn new material
2. Visualize:- imagery and visual techniques to reinforce your knowledge of the material.
3. Verbalize: utilizing words and writing to improve and accelerate your learning.
4. Simulate: using tools and people to simulate your learning in a variety of different ways like role-play
5. Perform
6. Repeat: continuously repeat the information you are learning in a variety of creative ways that will enhance your retention and recall of information.

Your Preferred Learning Style

•Visual Learner:This learner prefers learning through the use of colors, pictures and images.

•Aural Learner:This learner prefers learning through the use of sounds and music.

•Verbal Learner: This learner prefers learning through the use of words in the form of speech and writing.

•Physical Learner: This learner prefers learning through the physical use of their body and hands.

•Solitary Learner: This learner prefers learning through self-study methods that don’t involve other people.

•Social Learner: This learner prefers learning through working closely and interacting with other people.

•Logical Learner: This learner prefers learning through the use of reasoning and logic.
