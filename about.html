What is Sikchya?

Sikchya is a Educational site, which provides Information, News, Entertainment, and Latest events happening around the world. Sikchya serves links to different sites to make the viewers easier to navigate. The site also is an online digital library of education research and information.

Sickhya Group continues to explore new approaches to enhancing and expanding services to the user community. Through collaborative efforts and with feedback provided by the Sikchya user community, the Sikchya program continues to enhance online services, expand access to full-text materials, and increase the number of journal and non-journal sources indexed in the Collection.

For now, we are providing the link to the other sites and content. In futire we will have our own database and contents for our viewers.

Who is Behind Sikchya?

Sikchya was founded in 2012 by CEO Sagar Acharya, the pioneers in interactive Web-based user content. In the early-stage investment firm is planning to specializing in digital and interactive entertainment and learnings content.
